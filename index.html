WHO'S BEHIND IT

I'm Luke. I started training as the small kid — small in height, small in weight — trying to build size, confidence and a better game of football. The training worked, but what stuck was everything around it: what I ate, how I recovered, and how I kept showing up on the weeks life got loud. Heavy House started there, before I even knew it was starting.

Now I coach everyone from professional athletes to marathon runners to mums and dads — fat loss, strength, recomposition, or simply having someone in your corner. The common thread is honesty: no one-size-fits-all programs, no quick fixes, no bullshit. Fitness shouldn't cost you your friends, family or freedom — it should add to your life, not take from it.
